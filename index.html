Map of Greece

Language

The official and primary language spoken in Greece is Modern Greek. Over 97% of the population speaks it and it is used in government, education, and everyday life.

Language Family

Greek belongs to the Hellenic language family. It does not belong to the Romance, Germanic, or Slavic language families. Instead it forms its own branch of the Indo-European languages called the Hellenic branch.

Language Influences

Greek stayed mostly independent but borrowed some words from Latin, Turkish, Italian, and French throughout history.

Religion

The main religion in Greece today is Greek Orthodox Christianity. About 81–90% of the population identifies as Greek Orthodox.

Important Religious Influences

Ancient Greek polytheism (worship of many gods)

Christianity, especially the Greek Orthodox Church

Islam introduced during Ottoman rule
