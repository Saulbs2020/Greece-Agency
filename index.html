Greece Odyssey Travel

Discover the beauty, history, and culture of Greece. Travel through ancient cities, breathtaking islands, and delicious Mediterranean cuisine.

Athens

Visit the legendary Acropolis and Parthenon. Athens is one of the oldest cities in the world and the birthplace of democracy.

Santorini

Famous for its white houses and blue domes. Watch some of the most beautiful sunsets in the world.

Greek Food

Traditional Dishes Moussaka : A layered dish with eggplant, minced meat, potatoes, and béchamel sauce, baked to golden perfection.  Souvlaki – Small skewers of grilled meat often pork or chicken, usually served with pita, vegetables, and tzatziki sauce.  Spanakopita – A savory spinach and feta cheese pie wrapped in crisp phyllo pastry.  Famous Foods or Drinks  Metaxa – A famous Greek brandy blended with wine and herbs  Frappe – Cold instant coffee with foam, very popular in summer.  Tsipouro – it’s a strong grape-based spirit

Ancient History

Greece is home to ancient myths, gods like Zeus and Athena, and important historical discoveries that shaped the world.

Travel Tips

Best time to visit: April – October

Currency: Euro

Popular activities: Island hopping, beach visits, exploring ruins.
