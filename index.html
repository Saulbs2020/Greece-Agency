Greek Odyssey Travel

Explore the history, culture, language, and delicious food of Greece.

Country Information

Country: Greece

Capital: Athens

Map

Language

The official and primary language spoken in Greece is Modern Greek. Over 97% of the population speaks it, and it is used in government, education, and everyday life.

Language Family

The language spoken in Greece belongs to the Hellenic language family. It does not belong to the Romance, Germanic, or Slavic families. Instead, it forms its own branch of the Indo-European languages called the Hellenic branch.

Language Influences

Greek stayed mostly independent but borrowed words from Latin, Turkish, Italian, and French throughout history.

Religion

The main religion in Greece today is Greek Orthodox Christianity. About 81–90% of the population identifies as Greek Orthodox.

Important Religious Influences

1. Ancient Greek polytheism (worship of many gods)

2. Christianity, especially the Greek Orthodox Church

3. Islam, introduced during Ottoman rule

Greek Food

Traditional Dishes

Moussaka – A layered dish with eggplant, minced meat, potatoes, and béchamel sauce baked until golden.

Souvlaki – Small skewers of grilled meat (usually pork or chicken) served with pita, vegetables, and tzatziki sauce.

Spanakopita – A savory spinach and feta cheese pie wrapped in crispy phyllo pastry.

Famous Drinks

Metaxa – A famous Greek brandy blended with wine and herbs.

Frappe – Cold instant coffee with foam, very popular during summer.

Tsipouro – A strong grape-based alcoholic drink.
