Country

Country: Greece

Capital: Athens

Map


Language

The official language in Greece is Modern Greek. Over 97% of the population speaks it.

Language Family

Greek belongs to the Hellenic language family, its own branch of Indo-European languages.

Influences

Greek stayed mostly independent but borrowed words from Latin, Turkish, Italian, and French.


Religion

The main religion in Greece is Greek Orthodox Christianity. About 81–90% of people identify as Greek Orthodox.

Historical Influences

Ancient Greek polytheism

Christianity (Greek Orthodox Church)

Islam during Ottoman rule


Greek Food

Moussaka

A layered dish with eggplant, minced meat, potatoes, and béchamel sauce.

Souvlaki

Small skewers of grilled meat served with pita and tzatziki.

Spanakopita

A spinach and feta pie wrapped in crispy phyllo pastry.

Metaxa

A famous Greek brandy blended with wine and herbs.

Frappe

Cold instant coffee with foam.

Tsipouro

A strong grape-based spirit.
